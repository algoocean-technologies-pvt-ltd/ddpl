LEGACY

IN CHANGING TIMES… WHAT REMAINS
UNCHNAGED IS OUR FOCUS.

Built on a legacy of trust and with more than 4 decades of excellence, Dharmanandan is synonymous to competence and innovation in diamond manufacturing to cater customers worldwide with finest quality diamonds and jewellery.

The firm takes pride in ensuring their clients to have a rich and fruitful buying experience through their bespoke list of services curated for brand owners and distributors. Their commitment to business relationships is what gives them a competitive edge over others.

THE FIRM

CREATING VALUE FOR OUR CUSTOMERS IN OUR JOURNEY TO SUCCESS.

Regarded as one of the world’s largest diamond manufacturer, Dharmanandan Diamonds formerly known as Shreeji Gems, was founded by Laljibhai Patel & Tulsibhai Patel, in 1980. Shortly after, achieving the above tag of largest diamond manufacturer in the world in 0.30 carats to 5.00 carat size diamonds the firm rebranded as Dharmanandan Diamonds Pvt. Ltd.

in 2008, the company became the Sightholder of DeBeers Group of Companies and has implemented Best Practice Principles throughout the supply chain to produce malpractice free diamonds. Dharmanandan Diamonds Pvt. Ltd. has further developed itself by joining Forevermark Program and manufacturing the most ethical, rare and beautiful diamonds in the world.

CRAFTSMANSHIP

PASSIONATE TO CREATE THE MOST SOUGHT AFTER DIAMOND.

Our highly experienced team has worked with us over the years, and many times over generations of the family are working with us bringing continuity, expertise and quality to our entire collection.

For Dharmanandan Quality Is Everything.

Our craftsmen attend to each piece of diamond with great care and attention. Whilst each piece of diamond, whether bespoke or not, is given a certain number of hours in our workshop production schedule, from planning to final polishing. With an obsessive attention to detail, and a passion for creating the most beautiful diamonds in the world, our manufacturing capabilities blend with our highly skilled, talented craftsmen to produce diamonds for a global audience.

OUR ADVANTAGE

WE ARE EVER EVOLVING.

Dharmanandan Diamonds a manufacturer sourcing mined diamond directly from the mines they represent can offer their clients larger options of quality natural diamonds in stock, as per their clients demand to choose from. For their clients’ ‘compromise’ is not an option.

The key differentiating advantage of Dharmanandan Diamonds is that they cover all the processes from manufacturing to selling. This saves time and costs as their customer deal with the manufacturer directly. By playing multiple roles of Manufacturer, Stock keeper, Wholesaler, Retailer, Online seller and many more… have kept them one step ahead of the competition.

Main take away for their customers is the customer gets quality diamonds at a reasonable price.

With a distribution network spanning the globe, technology at the helm of creation of impeccable quality diamonds, certification from global bodies, ethical diamonds and best process principals have made them the first choice of diamond suppliers to brands world over.

Their industry defining online platform dharmHK.com, has made buying quality diamonds ethically sourced just a click away.

ASSOCIATIONS

DIAMONDS ARE RARE, PRECIOUS AND INHERENTLY VALUABLE BUT THEIR WORTH IS ECLIPSED BY THAT OF THE PEOPLE WHO WORK TOGETHER TO BRING THEM SAFELY TO THE LIGHT.

We at Dharmanandan have partnered with the world’s largest raw diamond producers, to maintain demand for, and confidence in diamonds, by promoting the integrity and reputation of the diamond industry and sharing best practices. By understanding the material socioeconomic and environmental benefits and impacts of diamond production. Further leverage this understanding to identify opportunities to minimise the impacts of the industry whilst maximizing the positive value created.
