OUR Milestones

THE FIRM

CREATING VALUE FOR OUR CUSTOMERS IN OUR JOURNEY TO SUCCESS.

Regarded as one of the world’s largest diamond manufacturer, Dharmanandan Diamonds formerly known as Shreeji Gems, was founded by Laljibhai Patel & Tulsibhai Patel, in 1980. Shortly after, achieving the above tag of largest diamond manufacturer in the world in 0.30 carats to 5.00 carat size diamonds the firm rebranded as Dharmanandan Diamonds Pvt. Ltd.

in 2008, the company became the Sightholder of DeBeers Group of Companies and has implemented Best Practice Principles throughout the supply chain to produce malpractice free diamonds. Dharmanandan Diamonds Pvt. Ltd. has further developed itself by joining Forevermark Program and manufacturing the most ethical, rare and beautiful diamonds in the world.

ASSOCIATIONS

DIAMONDS ARE RARE, PRECIOUS AND INHERENTLY VALUABLE BUT THEIR WORTH IS ECLIPSED BY THAT OF THE PEOPLE WHO WORK TOGETHER TO BRING THEM SAFELY TO THE LIGHT.

We at Dharmanandan have partnered with the world’s largest raw diamond producers, to maintain demand for, and confidence in diamonds, by promoting the integrity and reputation of the diamond industry and sharing best practices. By understanding the material socioeconomic and environmental benefits and impacts of diamond production. Further leverage this understanding to identify opportunities to minimise the impacts of the industry whilst maximizing the positive value created.

DeBeers Sightholder

It is one of the important factors for the diamond manufacturer to become the Sightholder. To become the Sightholder, the company must align all of the operations in line to the Best Practice Principles (BPP), which is the highest standards in the diamond industry for manufacturing. DDPL become Sightholder in 2008. DeBeers is one of the largest producers of rough diamonds in the world and Sightholder status provides the consistency in the rough supply which ultimately leads the business sustainability for the diamond manufacturer.

Alrosa Alliance

Alrosa Alliance represents our long-term partnership with Alrosa, one of the largest mine in the world. This long term partnership status is awarded to the diamond manufacturer who meet high standards of business conduct and possess a keen insight into the diamond market. Being Alrosa Alliance title identified as a priority buyer of gem quality rough diamonds and a responsible business partner.

BSI

Our Surat Premises is BSI certifies for the following standards:

  • Quality Management System - ISO 9001:2015

  • Occupational Health & Safety Management System – OHSAS 18001:2007

  • Environmental Management System – ISO 14001:2015

FOREVERMARK

Forevermark is a brand which certifies only 1% of the total diamonds produced in the world. DDPL produces the world’s most rare and beautiful diamonds with Forevermark brand to offer the exclusiveness to its customers.

American Gem Society (AGS)

AGS is the society of the elite diamond manufacturers and provides the grading service of the polished diamonds followed by stringent quality standards. DDPL is the proud member of American Gem Society.

Diamonds Do Good

Diamonds Do Good is a global nonprofit organization whose mission is supporting programs that develop and empower people in natural diamond communities. DDPL is official partner in Diamonds Do Good initiative in line with DDPL’s mission of CSR and advocates this initiative and supports it with all efforts made in this direction.
