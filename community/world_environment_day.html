World Environment Day

Today, any country facing the biggest challenge is not the terrorism but Global Warming. One should orient the action on World Environment Day to an activity that promotes sustainable consumption and be the one to reshape our future. We, at Dharmanandan, understand our responsibility and thus do our business in eco-friendly manner as well as creating awareness about eco-system in the society where we do business.
Dharmanandan Diamonds celebrates The World Environment Day every year at the Surat premises to be a part of initiatives we should take to save our renewable natural resources.

More than 100 peopletake oath to plant and care a tree on the occasion where every presented person is given a plant by the company.
