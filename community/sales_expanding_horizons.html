Sales Motivational Workshop - Expanding Horizons

In a fast changing world, certainly people required to adapt the changes. Dharmanandan Diamonds works hard to give its sales force the selling skills and motivation they required to create long-lasting change. As a part of the company’s Motivational Programs, the 3rd Sales Motivational Workshop – Expanding Horizons took placed on 11th January 2014 at IDI City Center, Surat.

Dharmanandan Diamonds always believes in creating the leaders within and its best justified by the title of the workshop, ‘Expanding Horizons’. About 110 Sales Executives of Dharmanandan Diamonds throughout the world have actively participated to ignite their mind on the way to success.

Two of the well-known mentors in the industry were presents to share the objective of the workshop. Mr. Sanjay Shah, in the first session, has discussed about the relationship building with the topic of ‘Our Words Create Our World’ and Mr. Aslam Charaniya has focused on variety of innovative ways for selling under the topic of ‘Carving Sales in 21st Century’.
The enthusiastic Director of company, Mr. Piyush Patel stated that the purpose of this event is not limited to business only, but it is generic for your life that can kick you into gear for any pickle you might be in.

With the presence of Dharmanandan’s Top Young Management, Mr. Hitesh Patel, MD of DDPL Group has mentioned that the world had changed. Business today is more challenging. It’s not okay to be just “good enough” anymore. Jam-packed full of proven techniques will ignite salesmanship within you and give you the mindset, the strategies and the techniques that you require to succeed.

At the end, Mr. Vipul Sutariya, Director of Marketing thanked both the mentors and addressed the sales team to focus on the gap between the knowledge and action. He added “Acting consistently and towards the right purpose is important. Work out your vision. Work out your purpose. Be clear on what the customer wants”.
